At InviZ, our mission is to create a user-friendly interactive website that informs the younger generation of the importance of world hunger through clear and concise data visualizations. This was done by presenting graphic representations of undernourishment, decline in crop growth, food deserts, and food waste. In a world of abundant wealth and resources, where enough food is produced to feed everyone on the planet, it is unacceptable that hundreds of millions of people suffer from hunger or food insecurity.

This website was developed through an in-person Kode With Klossy summer camp located in the greater Los Angeles area. Through this opportunity, three young women have made a functional website and data solutions dedicated to inform and create a call to action about world hunger.
